Khandelwal Samaj Raipur

The Khandelwal Samaj, Raipur, serves as the cornerstone of our community, dedicated to preserving the cultural identity and values of the Khandelwal Vaishya community in Chhattisgarh. Our purpose is to provide a central hub for social interaction, mutual assistance, and collective growth. Rooted in the principles of honesty, hard work, and philanthropy, the Samaj plays a vital role in organizing religious festivals, maintaining community assets, and supporting families in times of need. By bridging the gap between generations, we ensure that our rich traditions continue to thrive in a modern urban landscape.

Khandelwal Yuva Samaj

The Khandelwal Yuva Samaj is the dynamic youth wing of our organization, focused on mobilizing the energy and talent of our younger generation. Our role is to bridge the gap between tradition and innovation by organizing professional workshops, sports tournaments, and networking events. We are committed to social responsibility, frequently leading initiatives like blood donation drives and environmental awareness campaigns. By empowering the youth to take leadership roles, the Yuva Samaj ensures the long-term vitality and progress of our community in Raipur.

Our History

Tracing its roots back several decades, the Khandelwal Samaj in Raipur began as a small gathering of families seeking to maintain their cultural ties. Over the years, through the dedication of our elders and the "Bhamashah" spirit of our members, the organization has grown into a structured body with its own community halls and social infrastructure. From simple neighborhood meetings to grand state-level conventions, our history is a testament to the resilience and unity of the Khandelwal people in the heart of Chhattisgarh.
