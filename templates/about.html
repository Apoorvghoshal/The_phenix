About Khandelwal Samaj

The Khandelwal Yuva Samaj is the dynamic youth wing of our organization, focused on mobilizing the energy and talent of our younger generation. Our role is to bridge the gap between tradition and innovation by organizing professional workshops, sports tournaments, and networking events.

We are committed to social responsibility, frequently leading initiatives like blood donation drives and environmental awareness campaigns. By empowering the youth to take leadership roles, the Yuva Samaj ensures the long-term vitality and progress of our community in Raipur.

Khandelwal Yuva Mandal

The Khandelwal Yuva Samaj is the dynamic youth wing of our organization, focused on mobilizing the energy and talent of our younger generation. Our role is to bridge the gap between tradition and innovation by organizing professional workshops, sports tournaments, and networking events. We are committed to social responsibility, frequently leading initiatives like blood donation drives and environmental awareness campaigns. By empowering the youth to take leadership roles, the Yuva Samaj ensures the long-term vitality and progress of our community in Raipur.

The Yuva Mandal focuses on leadership development, community service, and encouraging youth participation to ensure a progressive future for the samaj.
